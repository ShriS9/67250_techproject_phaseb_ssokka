Mission Statement

Since 1993, we have sought to provide the public with access with wide range of contemporary art. We have placed a special emphasis on the role of technology in the creation, display, and research of art.

Milestones

  • 2025: Established the Center of Interactive Arts for digital installations
  • 2020: Hosted the Digital Art Symposium, featuring numerous prolific artists and critics
  • 2010: Creation of the Mellon Center for Digital Humanities
  • 2002: Aquisition of classic oil painting, "Boy With Apple"
  • 1993: MonoMuse opens its doors to the public
