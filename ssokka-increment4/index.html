Welcome to MonoMuse

MonoMuse is a cultural institution dedicated to exploring the intersection of art and technology, providing interactive installations and experiences for visitors. We hope that our museum will educate the public on how art, technology and culture are inexorably linked, and inspire creators to experiment with their art in this digital age.


Since 1993, Monomuse has been one of the most versatile research and cultural institutions. Through our grants, exhibitions, and projects, we have pushed the bounds of contemporary art. We strive to reinvent ourselves year after year, and invite members of the public to give valuable feedback to our museum experience. We appreciate our members and supporters.

Some of our projects include public installations in downtown Pittsburgh, funding of teaching artists from Carnegie Mellon University, and our artist-in-residency program for those interested in merging traditional media with technology. Our exhibitions range from oil paintings to proto-holograms; we welcome an eclectic variety of pieces in our collection, and are constantly striving to add newer and "weirder" pieces.
